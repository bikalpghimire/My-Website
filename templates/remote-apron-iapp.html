Remote Apron & Isolated Aircraft Parking Position (IAPP)

Airport Design

Overview

Planning and geometric design of a remote aircraft apron and an Isolated Aircraft Parking Position (IAPP), including connecting taxiways and GSE road, ensuring ICAO standards.

Key Responsibilities & Contribution

  • Sized stands for the critical aircraft, set stand centerlines, and equipment clearances; laid out taxilane geometry and fillets.
  • Prepared Obstacle Limitation Surfaces (OLS) and checked the clearances for high masts, buildings, aprons, etc.
  • Developed pavement marking and lighting concepts per ICAO/FAA guidance.
  • Conceptual design of Storm Water Drainage System along with grading of Runway and Taxiway Strip
